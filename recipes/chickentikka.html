Chicken Tikka

Ingredients

  • boneless chicken
  • thick yogurt
  • kashmiri chilli powder
  • cumin powder
  • coriander powder
  • garam masala powder
  • lemon
  • butter
  • oil
  1. Take chicken pieces in a bowl. use boneless chicken pieces in this recipe. It is best to use chicken thigh instead of chicken breast which makes the dish more juicy.
  2. Now for tenderizing the meat, add in some lemon juice. Lemon juice helps making the chicken moist and juicy.
  3. Add in thick yogurt, ginger and garlic paste, oil or ghee in the bowl.
  4. Add in turmeric powder, chilli powder. If you use kashmiri chilli powder you might get red colour.
  5. Add in coriander powder, cumin powder and garam masala powder for marination.
  6. Season chicken with salt.
  7. Massage the chicken really well with spices, yogurt, lemon juice. Let the chicken marinade for few hours. You can let the chicken marinate overnight in fridge.
  8. For chicken kebab, i used onions, capsicum, tomatoes for skewering along with chicken. Take tomatoes in a chopping board, remove the seeds using a spoon from the tomatoes so it doesn't leave out water.
